### LLVM/Clang/LLDB/LLD

本节内容来自 [LLVM Weekly 第 647 期](http://llvmweekly.org/issue/647)，
[Alex Bradbury](https://www.linkedin.com/in/alex-bradbury/) 大哥持续稳定输出。

* 欢迎参加： The Beijing LLVM meetup [will take place on May 29th](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/beijing-llvm-meetup-may-29-2026/90888) and feature two RISC-V focused talks.

* 主持： Aaron Ballman is seeking to organise [a testimonial to ISO on the importance of open access to ISO standards documents](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/rfc-open-access-to-standards-documents/90856) on behalf of the LLVM community. Per the timeline, please leave comments and recommended edits on the linked draft through to May 27th.

* 简化： Reid Kleckner proposed [transitioning from reST to Markdown for LLVM documentation](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/rfc-make-myst-markdown-the-llvm-docs-format-rip-rest/90840).  There's a lot of support for this direction, but also some concerns about the churn, packaging issues, and the volume of existing reST documentation.

* 祝贺 Luke！ Luke Lau shares that [lnt.llvm.org is now tracking RISC-V performance for clang and GCC](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/lnt-llvm-org-now-tracking-risc-v-performance-for-clang-and-gcc/90824).  Now that the official LLVM-hosted LNT instance is back up, reporting has moved from Igalia's instance. Even more importantly, Luke gives a really great quick start guide on how to navigate the web interface and interpret the results.

* 感觉可能是个深远的变化？ Varad Rahul Kamthe posted an RFC on [supporting default arguments in LLVM intrinsics](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/rfc-tablegen-default-argument-support-for-llvm-intrinsics/90839) as a way to help avoid breaking changes when new arguments are added to intrinsics.

* 日积跬步： 32-bit RISC-V support was added to BOLT.
  [ce002b2](https://github.com/llvm/llvm-project/commit/ce002b263389).

* 欢迎 FreeBSD，最近一年看接触到了更多的 FreeBSD 伙伴： Initial FreeBSD support was added to LLVM's libc.
  [633539b](https://github.com/llvm/llvm-project/commit/633539bfa151).
