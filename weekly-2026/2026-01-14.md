### LLVM/Clang/LLDB/LLD

本节内容来自 [LLVM Weekly 第 628 期](http://llvmweekly.org/issue/628)，
[Alex Bradbury](https://www.linkedin.com/in/alex-bradbury/) 大哥持续稳定输出。

* 贡献和改进的机会： Nikita Popov wrote up a blog post on [LLVM: the bad parts](https://www.npopov.com/2026/01/11/LLVM-The-bad-parts.html), noting "This is not a list of reasons to not use LLVM, it’s a list of opportunities to improve LLVM".

* 来了： LLVM 22.x [will be branching on Tuesday 13th January](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/llvm-22-x-will-branch-next-week-tue-13th-jan/89377).

* 感谢分享： Reid Kleckner shared [results from benchmarking a static vs dynamic link build and test](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/rfc-llvm-link-llvm-dylib-should-default-to-on-on-posix-platforms/85908/64), including with ld.bfd.

* 有人用起来了么？ Yunbo Ni shared [initial results from a research project on 'targeted testing' for LLVM](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/investigating-targeted-testing-for-llvm-optimizations/89403), involving focused test generation for specific parts of LLVM.

* 所有程序员都需要注意： The LLVM Project Council reported that [the LLVM AI tool policy proposal is now accepted](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/rfc-llvm-ai-tool-policy-human-in-the-loop/89159/24).

* 底层系统软件的交织： Yonghong Song suggested [a change to ThinLTO to benefit kernel live patching](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/rfc-avoid-functions-like-foo-llvm-for-kernel-live-patch/89400), specifically that static functions like `<foo>()` shouldn't be promoted to `<foo>.llvm.<...>()`.

* 这是一个新环境的开始： Basic Mach-O triple support was added for RISC-V.
  [75d0251](https://github.com/llvm/llvm-project/commit/75d025124aa3).
