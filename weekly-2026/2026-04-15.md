### LLVM/Clang/LLDB/LLD

本节内容来自 [LLVM Weekly 第 641 期](http://llvmweekly.org/issue/641)，
[Alex Bradbury](https://www.linkedin.com/in/alex-bradbury/) 大哥持续稳定输出。

* 本周 EuroLLVM 2026 在爱尔兰都柏林举行，超过200人参会。

* 好文章： Kavin Gnanapandithan blogged about [tracking down and fixing a performance regression in LLVM RISC-V code generation](https://blog.kaving.me/blog/tracking-down-a-25-regression-on-llvm-risc-v/). It walks through the whole process of spotting a regression on Igalia's LNT instance, analysing the problem, and finding and patching the issue.

* 宋教授！ Fangrui Song wrote about [recent performance improvements to the LLD ELF linker](https://maskray.me/blog/2026-04-12-recent-lld-elf-performance-improvements). Comparing current `main` against LLVM 22.1 "Release+Asserts clang --gc-sections link is 1.37x as fast as lld 22.1; Chromium debug with --gdb-index is 1.07x as fast." Read the post for more details, as well as why `mold` and `wild` are faster still.
