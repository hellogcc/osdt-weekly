### LLVM/Clang/LLDB/LLD

本节内容来自 [LLVM Weekly 第 654 期](http://llvmweekly.org/issue/654)，
[Alex Bradbury](https://www.linkedin.com/in/alex-bradbury/) 大哥持续稳定输出。

* 有伙伴去么，冒个泡？ The call for papers for the toolchains track at the Linux Plumbers Conference [remains open until 24th July](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/cfp-toolchains-track-at-linux-plumbers-conf-26/91088).

* 郑同学持续发力： Yingwei Zheng shared a [progress update on the llvm-ub-aware interpreter](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/rfc-upstreaming-llvm-ub-aware-interpreter/89645/14).

* 可以关注下： Sergey Shcherbinin [answered a series of questions about their profile-guided software loop data prefetcher proposal](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/rfc-universal-profile-guided-automatic-software-loop-data-prefetcher-for-llvm/90916/5).

* Markdown 貌似要统一全球： the LangRef was rewritten as Markdown.
  [2cb32e4](https://github.com/llvm/llvm-project/commit/2cb32e4e72ce).

* 此生分明了： LLVM's dependency on Python was clarified.
  [8a4a2ae](https://github.com/llvm/llvm-project/commit/8a4a2aed7d75).

* 日积跬步： Ben Joffe's fast date conversion algorithm was implemented in LLVM's libc.
  [2705f46](https://github.com/llvm/llvm-project/commit/2705f4613aee).

* 日积跬步： A RISC-V architecture plugin for trap validation was added to LLDB.
  [c5026e4](https://github.com/llvm/llvm-project/commit/c5026e4e3332).
