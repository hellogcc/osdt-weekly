### LLVM/Clang/LLDB/LLD

本节内容来自 [LLVM Weekly 第 650 期](http://llvmweekly.org/issue/650)，
[Alex Bradbury](https://www.linkedin.com/in/alex-bradbury/) 大哥持续稳定输出。

* 看看： Herb Sutter shared a [trip report from the June ISO C++ standards meeting in Brno](https://herbsutter.com/2026/06/13/brno-trip-report/).

* 欢迎加入： The LLVM Foundation is [inviting applications for new board members](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/llvm-foundation-seeking-new-board-members-apply-by-july-15/91074).

* 日益急迫的问题： Aiden Grossman, Yunbo Ni, and Yingwei Zheng started an RFC discussion on [providing infrastructure for AI review of PRs](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/rfc-upstream-infrastructure-for-ai-review-of-prs/91042).

* 挺好的： Farid Zakaria is [setting up a working group on solving the challenges of linking massive binaries in LLD](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/rfc-forming-a-massive-binaries-working-group-in-lld/91031).  "The group would provide a platform for discussing cross-cutting concerns around relocation overflows, and modifications to the x86-64 ABI via code-models such as range-extension thunks."

* 不错的总结： Four months comes around quickly - it's time for another [update on Flang development](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/flang-liaison-report-to-j3/68468/12).  As usual, this is a great high level summary of where things are at and what has changed in the past four months.

* 有意思： Alex Zinenko is seeking agreement within the MLIR contributor community on [what to do with third-party project 'promotion' posts](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/rfc-what-to-do-with-third-party-project-promotion-posts/91011).

* 含金量！ The SpacemiT X60 scheduling model is now used for `-mtune=generic` for RISC-V. [5e8d406](https://github.com/llvm/llvm-project/commit/5e8d4064bc74).

* RISC-V P 扩展，不容易： `riscv_packed_simd.h` was added containing RISC-V P extension intrinsics.
  [b000f90](https://github.com/llvm/llvm-project/commit/b000f9032911).

* 日积跬步： LLVM's libc gained an `LIBC_MATH_ASSUME_ROUND_NEAREST_ONLY` build option.
  [582643f](https://github.com/llvm/llvm-project/commit/582643f1ec62).

* RISC-V 日积跬步： LLDB learned to handle RISC-V feature attributes from ELF.
  [127a4c1](https://github.com/llvm/llvm-project/commit/127a4c1a883d).
