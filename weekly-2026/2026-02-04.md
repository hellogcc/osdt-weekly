### LLVM/Clang/LLDB/LLD

本节内容来自 [LLVM Weekly 第 631 期](http://llvmweekly.org/issue/631)，
[Alex Bradbury](https://www.linkedin.com/in/alex-bradbury/) 大哥持续稳定输出。

* 宋教授笔耕不缀： Fangrui Song wrote about [LLD changes in the upcoming LLVM 22 release](https://maskray.me/blog/2026-02-01-lld-22-elf-changes).

* 社区治理持续改进： LLVM Community Area Team Elections are [open for voting](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/2026-llvm-community-area-team-elections-voting-is-open/89586) until February 9th. Thank you to everyone who put themselves forward.

* 北京 LLVM Meetup 开始恢复： A Beijing LLVM x RISC-V meetup [will take place in Beijing on February 6th](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/beijing-llvm-meetup/89619).

* 好事儿： Petr Hosek suggests [adding a new PR label that can be applied to a PR that was landed unapproved, to make someone reviewed it](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/rfc-label-for-coordinating-post-commit-reviews/89665).

* 郑同学！ Yingwei Zheng would like to [upstream an undefined behaviour aware LLVM IR interpreter](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/rfc-upstreaming-llvm-ub-aware-interpreter/89645), which amongst other things would aid in reducing miscompilation reproducers.  So far there is strong support, alongside questions of what happens to the existing ExecutionEngine interpreter.

* “情绪引擎”是个啥： Support (CPU definitions, a hardware errata fix, and exposing the correct set of supported instructions) was added for the Mips R5900 CPU used for the 'Emotion Engine' processor in the PlayStation 2.  [f4b593a](https://github.com/llvm/llvm-project/commit/f4b593a792a6).

* 打开了新的想象力； RISC-V's assembler gained support for Mach-O relocations.
  [460c9b2](https://github.com/llvm/llvm-project/commit/460c9b2db144).
