### LLVM/Clang/LLDB/LLD

本节内容来自 [LLVM Weekly 第 653 期](http://llvmweekly.org/issue/653)，
[Alex Bradbury](https://www.linkedin.com/in/alex-bradbury/) 大哥持续稳定输出。

* 荣誉！ The new issue of Communications of the ACM has a piece from Vikram S Adve and Chris Lattner on [the LLVM compiler infrastructure, the impact it's had, and the effect of the federal research funding it received](https://cacm.acm.org/federal-funding-of-academic-research/the-llvm-compiler-infrastructure/).

* LLVM 23 要来了： LLVM 23.x [will branch next week](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/llvm-23-x-will-branch-next-week-tue-14th-july/91233), on Tuesday 14th July.

* LLDB 的开发最近越来越活跃了： Satyajanga kicked off an RFC discussion on [address spaces support in LLDB](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/rfc-address-spaces-support-in-lldb/91222).

* 可以看看：I somehow missed it when first posted, but Joseph Huber proposed [a concurrency sanitizer for AMD GPU targets](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/rfc-a-thread-concurrency-sanitizer-for-gpus-in-compiler-rt/91113) and has now [followed up with some benchmarks and PRs](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/rfc-a-thread-concurrency-sanitizer-for-gpus-in-compiler-rt/91113/6).

* 注意这个小变化： lit process output is now truncated to 10KiB by default.
  [524b97a](https://github.com/llvm/llvm-project/commit/524b97ad3332).
