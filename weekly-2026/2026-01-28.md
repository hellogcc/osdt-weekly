### LLVM/Clang/LLDB/LLD

本节内容来自 [LLVM Weekly 第 630 期](http://llvmweekly.org/issue/630)，
[Alex Bradbury](https://www.linkedin.com/in/alex-bradbury/) 大哥持续稳定输出。

* 请关注： Voting should open very soon for the 2026 LLVM Community Area Team Elections, running through to February 9th. Keep an eye on [the LLVM announcements category on Discourse](https://discourse.llvm.org/c/announce/46).

* 欧洲LLVM开发者会议开始买票了： Registration is [now open for EuroLLVM 2026](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/2026-eurollvm-developers-meeting-registration-open/89557).  Register by 1st March for the early bird rate. The conference will take place on April 14th-15th in Dublin, with pre-conference events on April 13th.

* 这周末/下周： For those of you at FOSDEM, be sure to check out the [LLVM dev room on Saturday](https://fosdem.org/2026/schedule/track/llvm/). It looks like a really interesting program that I'm looking forward to catching up on once videos are posted.

* 太好了是宋教授： Fangrui Song wrote about [long branches in compilers, assemblers, and linkers](https://maskray.me/blog/2026-01-25-long-branches-in-compilers-assemblers-and-linkers).

* 非常好的总结： My Igalia colleague Luke Lau has posted a [2nd part to his exploration of RISC-V LLVM performance improvements](https://lukelau.me/2026/01/26/closing-the-gap-pt2.html), this time walking through improvements to loop vectoriser cost modelling that lead to a 7% gain in a particular SPEC benchmark.

* 本周 LLVM IR 的改动讨论非常多： Nuno Lopes, Juneyoung Lee, Pedro Lobo, and George Mitenkov posted an RFC on [adding a new byte type to LLVM IR](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/rfc-add-a-new-byte-type-to-llvm-ir/89522) to represent raw memory data in registers and enable the eventual removal of undef from LLVM IR.

* 本周 LLVM IR 的改动讨论非常多： Abhilash Majumder started an RFC discussion on [allowing LLVM's control flow graph infrastructure to work with PTX](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/rfc-ptx-llvm-cfg-feature-for-pure-ptx-instructions/89556).  Nikita Popov [suggested](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/rfc-ptx-llvm-cfg-feature-for-pure-ptx-instructions/89556/3) that framing this as "CFG for MC" and making it not PTX specific may be a path forwards.

* 本周 LLVM IR 的改动讨论非常多： Sam Elliott started a discussion on [disabling clang-format for TableGen within the LLVM tree](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/clang-format-and-tablegen-in-llvm-tree/89526) given that most TableGen in tree appears not to use it. This was agreed, and issues filed for improving clang-format's output with TableGen.

* CHERI真是个大工程： Some initial patches for RVY (RISC-V CHERI) MC layer support started to land. [5eb3969](https://github.com/llvm/llvm-project/commit/5eb3969c7a0b).

* 本周 LLVM IR 的改动讨论非常多：  In addition to all the other additions mentioned above, the specification for float min/max operations has been clarified/changed after a very lengthy RFC discussion.  [6bae2a9](https://github.com/llvm/llvm-project/commit/6bae2a98b4c7).

* 日积跬步： A weak AVL tree implementation was added to LLVM's libc and used to implement `tsearch`.
  [db71332](https://github.com/llvm/llvm-project/commit/db713325d530).
