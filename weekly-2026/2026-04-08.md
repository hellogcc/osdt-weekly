### LLVM/Clang/LLDB/LLD

本节内容来自 [LLVM Weekly 第 640 期](http://llvmweekly.org/issue/640)，
[Alex Bradbury](https://www.linkedin.com/in/alex-bradbury/) 大哥持续稳定输出。

* 所有开源社区都正在面对的问题： Jakub Kuderski started a discussion thread on [concerns about the number of AI-generated bug fixes being submitted](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/concerns-about-influx-of-ai-generated-bug-fixes/90381). It's a lengthy thread with a lot of viewpoints expressed that I feel I couldn't do justice to summarise. But even where there are different views on the solution, there is plenty of concern about maintainer load.

* 国内香山团队的伙伴？ "buggfg" started a discussion on the [idea of 'RVCC' an incubator for high-performance RISC-V compiler optimisations](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/rfc-rvcc-an-llvm-incubator-for-high-performance-risc-v-compiler-optimizations/90376). In general there re some concerns about producing an LLVM fork and wondering whether other methods might achieve similar results (and suggestions to discuss further through the biweekly RISC-V contributor calls, or in external groups).

* 欢迎围观： Notes from the 1st April LLVM Project Council meeting [are now available](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/project-council-meeting-april-1-2026/90420).

* 欢迎围观： LLVM Foundation board minutes from March [are now available](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/board-meeting-minutes-march-6-2026/90445).

* MLIR的风还是吹向了 JavaScript： Zhixun Tan and a long list of collaborators proposed [JSIR: A high-level IR ofr JavaScript](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/rfc-jsir-a-high-level-ir-for-javascript/90456). Built in MLIR, it notably supports high fiedlity round-trips between source to/from AST to/from JSIR.

### 本周推荐阅读

本周推荐：《漫长的战败》

作者：桥本明子

译者：李鹏程

推荐：小编1号

去年，代表日本极右翼思想高市早苗上台之后，中日关系遭遇了重大的倒退，并且已经开始影响到两国在多项技术领域的交流。去年和今年在日本东京的 RISC-V Tokyo Day，从中国大陆前往的参展商和参会者都出现了可感知的下降。在这个局面下，重新拿起这本《漫长的战败： 日本的文化创伤、记忆与认同》，对于理解目前日本作为一个政权国家的走向有着指导性的意义。作者展示了历史的复杂性，对各个维度和历史阶段的日本各派别表现进行了描述。
