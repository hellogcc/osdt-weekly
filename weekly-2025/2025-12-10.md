### LLVM/Clang/LLDB/LLD

本节内容来自 [LLVM Weekly 第 623 期](http://llvmweekly.org/issue/623)，
[Alex Bradbury](https://www.linkedin.com/in/alex-bradbury/) 大哥持续稳定输出。

* 截稿时间1月11日： The call for proposals is now [open for EuroLLVM 2026](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/2026-eurollvm-developers-meeting-call-for-proposals/89059).  Submit your proposal by 11th January 2026. EuroLLVM will take place in April in Dublin.

* USLLVMDev2025 的播放列表： Additional recordings from the 2025 US LLVM Developers' Meeting have been appearing on YouTube and they are now [collected together in a handy playlist](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL_R5A0lGi1AC_83nImBFdgkyOzWz_02Gc).

* 提高了很多： My Igalia colleague Mikhail R. Gadelha has written up a blog post version of a talk delivered at the RISC-V Summit earlier this year about [improving LLVM generated code performance for RISC-V](https://blogs.igalia.com/compilers/2025/11/22/unlocking-15-more-performance-a-case-study-in-llvm-optimization-for-risc-v/).

* 重要的： Alina Sbirlea and others are [establishing a working group on formal specification for LLVM IR](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/rfc-forming-a-working-group-on-formal-specification-for-llvm/89056).  The kickoff meeting already took place earlier today, but a recurring meeting will be added to the LLVM calendar and I'm sure notes will be shared.

* 微改进： The RISC-V backend learned to use the Zicond conditional move instructions for floating point selects once Zfinx/Zdinx is present (i.e. when there is no separate FPR so GPRs are used for floats).  [2e21bb8](https://github.com/llvm/llvm-project/commit/2e21bb815d52).

* 基于 LLVM libc 的 Linux 发行版或许指日可待： LLVM's libc now has finer grained control over the selection of higher performance function implementations.
  [8701c2a](https://github.com/llvm/llvm-project/commit/8701c2a9106b).

* LLDB 默默努力： A new `breakpoint add` subcommand was added to LLDB, aiming to supplant `breakpoint set`.
  [2110db0](https://github.com/llvm/llvm-project/commit/2110db0f4959).

### 本周推荐阅读

本周推荐：《做自己，不忐忑》

作者：龚琳娜

推荐：小编1号

最近看到龚琳娜是因为鲁豫的博客节目，而顺着节目摸到了这本《做自己，不忐忑》。这本书对于我而言最大的价值就是让我清晰和强烈的进入到了另一个人的生活，看到了另外一种完全不同的生活是什么样子的。作者的文字如此的坦诚，以至于读者甚至会开始担心这种坦诚的自我暴露是否构成了一种需要帮助的表现。而鲁豫和龚琳娜的对谈非常的及时，让我同时感受到了看待同一段人生的多重视角。

确实，已经没有什么好忐忑的了。
