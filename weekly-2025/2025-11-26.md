### LLVM/Clang/LLDB/LLD

本节内容来自 [LLVM Weekly 第 621 期](http://llvmweekly.org/issue/621)，
[Alex Bradbury](https://www.linkedin.com/in/alex-bradbury/) 大哥持续稳定输出。

* 看起来很有用： Abdullah Amin wrote on the LLVM project blog about [their GSoC project to extend LLDB with a rich disassembler](https://blog.llvm.org/posts/2025-08-gsoc-lldb-rich-disassembler/).

* 可以看到回放了： Recordings from the 2025 US LLVM Developer's Meeting have started to appear on YouTube. They're not collected in a playlist yet, but for now just see the [LLVM video list](https://www.youtube.com/@LLVMPROJ/videos).

* 欢迎报名： Tanya Lattner is [seeking volunteers for the EuroLLVM Developers Meeting Program Committee](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/2026-eurollvm-developers-meeting-volunteer-for-the-program-committee/88884).

* 社区快速增长： The Clang area team are [proposing to grow the team size to 5 in 2026](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/expanding-clang-area-team-size-to-5-in-2026/88896).  "We think the Clang area team would benefit from having more folks to take on action items, read RFCs in detail, do more follow-up, and do more to shepherd RFCs along. There were many talented, qualified candidates last year, and we’d like to rotate new folks onto the area team to have fresh faces, get more folks engaged, diversify the domain expertise on the area team, and empower more people to get stuff done."

* 看不全懂，但是可以看看： Round table notes were shared on [lifetimes](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/lifetimes-round-table-notes/88899), [C++ interoperability](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/c-interoperability-round-table-notes/88902), ["operational maturity"](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/operational-maturity-round-table-notes/88898), and [annotations for C++ interoperability](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/annotations-for-c-interoperability-round-table-notes/88943).  Additionally, new [LLVM project council meeting notes](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/llvm-project-council-meeting-november-19-2025/88848) discuss SFrame support and requiring pull requests for all llvm-project commits.

* 这个有意思： Alex Susu started an RFC discussion on [upstreaming a backend for the Connex vector accelerator](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/rfc-adding-back-end-for-the-connex-vector-accelerator-as-an-experimental-target/88910).

* 好： Tablegen infrastructure was added to support pretty printing arguments in LLVM intrinsics, for instance printing a human readable name alongside the argument.
  [39e7712](https://github.com/llvm/llvm-project/commit/39e7712ac520).

* 日积跬步： A RISC-V Zilsd load/store pair optimisation pass was implemented.
  [645e0dc](https://github.com/llvm/llvm-project/commit/645e0dcbff33).

### 本周推荐阅读

本周推荐：《黄油：一部丰富的历史》

作者： 伊莱恩·科斯罗瓦

译者： 赵祖华

推荐： 小编1号

作为华北到江浙平原地带成长起来的人，从小对黄油的记忆并不突出。长大之后才发现原来黄油在全球范围内作为人类的发现已经超越千年。到现在也还有手工/家庭制作的黄油，从奶牛/牦牛/羊的乳汁中反复碰撞得到的黄油，在全球享有多个博物馆讲述自己的历史。

作者的文体优美，描写牛吃青草和反刍都能让我看饿了。
