### LLVM/Clang/LLDB/LLD

本节内容来自 [LLVM Weekly 第 625 期](http://llvmweekly.org/issue/625)，
[Alex Bradbury](https://www.linkedin.com/in/alex-bradbury/) 大哥持续稳定输出。

* 有去的伙伴么？ The program for the FOSDEM 2026 LLVM dev room [has been published](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/fosdem-2026-llvm-dev-room-program-is-published/89162).

* 这个世界上又多了一个双周报： Tue Ly is [starting up a new biweekly LLVM libc math library meeting](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/fortnightly-llvm-libc-math-library-meeting/89166).

* 各位用过这个工具么？ Jin Huang proposed [upstreaming the Propeller profile conversion tool](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/rfc-upstreaming-propeller-profile-conversion-tool/89173).

* 我感觉用的很少： Nikita Popov [returned to the topic of removing subproject tarballs from future LLVM releases](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/rfc-do-something-with-the-subproject-tarballs-in-the-release-page/75024/10).

* 所有使用AI辅助的开发者都需要关注： Reid Kleckner shared [a new iteration of the proposed LLVM AI tool policy](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/rfc-llvm-ai-tool-policy-human-in-the-loop/89159/1).

* 在Linux内核那边争议很大： Initial codegen support for big-endian RISC-V was added.
  [fefda86](https://github.com/llvm/llvm-project/commit/fefda86c28f6).
