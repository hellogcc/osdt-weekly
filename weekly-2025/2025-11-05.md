### LLVM/Clang/LLDB/LLD

本节内容来自 [LLVM Weekly 第 618 期](http://llvmweekly.org/issue/618)，
[Alex Bradbury](https://www.linkedin.com/in/alex-bradbury/) 大哥持续稳定输出。

* 看看： Min Hsu published a [second blog post on LLVM's Machine Scheduler](https://myhsu.xyz/llvm-machine-scheduler-2/).

* 2026年2月1日，一起去啊！ The call for proposals for the 2026 FOSDEM LLVM dev room [is now open](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/cfp-fosdem-2026-llvm-dev-room/88746) and will remain open until November 30th.

* 密集的学习，跟上社区的讨论速度： Various notes from roundtables at the LLVM Dev Meeting have been shared: [AI policy](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/ai-policy-roundtable-meeting-notes/88718), [RISC-V](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/risc-v-roundtable-meeting-notes/88752/), [loop optimisation](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/loop-optimisations-roundtable-notes/88751).  Slides from the LLVM hearts ML workshop are [also now available](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/llvm-dev25-llvm-ml-workshop/87488/6).

* 参与讨论下？ Based on discussions at the LLVM Dev Meeting, Krzysztof Drewniak posted an MLIR RFC on [properties and attributes](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/rfc-properties-and-attributes-where-we-can-go-from-here/88745).  "At a high-level, the current system for non-attribute properties is a bit too incremental and a bit hard to introspect. However, one realization is that both current attributes and current non-attribute properties are kinds of data you can have in your program / attach to an operation. That is, we can re-root the Attribute hierarchy under a more general Property hierarchy...".

* 这个好，我正在用macOS： Shreeyash Pandey posted an RFC on [porting LLVM libc to MacOS](https://discourse.llvm.org/t/rfc-porting-llvm-libc-to-macos/88767).
